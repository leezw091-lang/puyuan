Our Story

Rooted in
China's Textile
Heartland

Founded in 2008 in Tongxiang, Zhejiang — the internationally acclaimed home textile capital of China — Puyuan has grown from a dedicated weaving workshop into a full-service export manufacturer trusted by buyers from Europe to the Middle East.

Every metre of fabric we produce carries the legacy of a region where sofa fabric production alone wraps the Earth's equator more than ten times every year.
